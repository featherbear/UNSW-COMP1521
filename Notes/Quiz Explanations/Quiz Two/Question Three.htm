0x55 -> 16^1 * 5 + 16^0 * 5

     -> 16 * 5 + 1 * 5

     -> 80 + 5

     -> 85

Convert dec:85 into binary...

0b01010101

 

0xAA -> 16^1 * 10 + 16^0 * 10

     -> 16 * 10 + 1 * 10

     -> 160 + 10

     -> 170

Convert dec:170 into binary...

0b10101010

 

 

(0x55 ^ 0xAA)

  0b01010101

^ 0b10101010

    --------

    11111111

 

result ^ 0xAA

 

  0b11111111

^ 0b10101010

    --------

    01010101
